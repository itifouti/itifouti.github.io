Ishak Tifouti

Postdoctoral researcher — Inria Makutu team, Pau, France
Inria center of Université de Bordeaux, Talence, France

Research Publications Teaching CV

I work on projection-based nonlinear adaptive model order reduction methods for parameterized partial differential equations, with applications to inverse problems in seismology and compressible flows.

Research interests

  • Reduced-order modeling
  • Metric-based mesh adaptation
  • Error estimation
  • Adjoint-based methods
  • Simulation of compressible flows
  • Harmonic full-wave inversion
