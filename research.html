Research

My research focuses on the development, analysis, and implementation of nonlinear model order reduction techniques for parameterized PDEs.

Projection-Based Nonlinear Model Reduction

Registration-based model reduction methods with spatio-parameter adaptivity for advection-dominated problems.

Mesh Adaptation

Anisotropic mesh adaptation strategies coupled with nonlinear reduced-order models for hyperbolic and compressible flow problems.

Inverse Problems

Reduced-order modeling techniques for inverse problems, with applications to wave propagation and fluid mechanics.
